## 8. Interpretation

### ES — Centralized hub-and-spoke with fused IDE/catalog

- `www.idee.es` acts as both the national IDE portal and the GeoNetwork catalog, concentrating 362 crawled pages in one domain.
- The catalog directly links to **9 service domains**, predominantly WMS/WMTS endpoints operated by the national ministry (`mapama.gob.es`).
- Regional IDEs (14 autonomous community catalogs) are linked from the national catalog but their own service endpoints were not crawled deeply.
- The 3-layer IDE→GeoNetwork→Service structure is **implicit**: the IDE and GeoNetwork occupy the same node, and services are one hop away.

### CH — Federated multi-layer with explicit catalog separation

- `www.geo.admin.ch` (IDE_NACIONAL) and `www.geocat.ch` (GEONETWORK) are **separate nodes**, making the IDE→Catalog separation structurally visible.
- The catalog links to **12 service domains** across multiple cantonal operators (WMS/WFS by canton: Basel, Schaffhausen, Zug, Jura, St. Gallen, etc.).
- `geodienste.ch` acts as a **cantonal service aggregation platform** (SERVICE_PLATFORM), a layer not present in ES.
- The 3-layer structure is **explicit and federated**: IDE → GeoNetwork → cantonal services.

### Key structural difference

| Dimension | ES (IDEE) | CH (geocat.ch) |
|---|---|---|
| IDE ↔ GeoNetwork | **Fused** in one domain | **Separated** (distinct nodes) |
| Service model | National ministry WMS/WMTS | Cantonal WMS/WFS + platform (geodienste) |
| Service diversity | Low (2 WMS/WMTS domains) | High (9 WMS/WFS/WMTS domains) |
| Catalog-to-service hops | 1 (catalog → service) | 1 (catalog → service, parallel) |
| Federation visible in graph | No | Yes (geo.admin.ch → geocat.ch → cantons) |
